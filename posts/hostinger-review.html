What is Hostinger?

Hostinger is a Lithuanian web hosting company founded in 2004. With over 3 million customers worldwide, it's grown from a budget host into a genuinely competitive platform offering shared hosting, VPS, cloud hosting, and WordPress hosting.

The big question: can a host this cheap actually deliver good performance? We set up a real WordPress site and ran it on Hostinger's Premium Shared plan for 60 days to find out.

Every plan includes a free domain for the first year and a free SSL certificate. That's $15–20 of value included in the price.

WordPress Optimisation

LiteSpeed Cache is pre-installed and configured for WordPress sites. Combined with their LiteSpeed servers, WordPress sites load noticeably faster than on competing hosts.
