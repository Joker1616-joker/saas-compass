<h1 style="font-family:'Syne',sans-serif;font-size:2.5rem;font-weight:800;margin-bottom:1rem">About SaaSCompass</h1>
<p>SaaSCompass was built by a small team of software professionals who got tired of reading biased, pay-to-rank "reviews" that never actually tested the products.</p>
<p>We test every tool we review. Real accounts, real use cases, real results.</p>
<h2>How We Make Money</h2>
<p>We earn affiliate commissions when you purchase through our links — at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site free. We never accept payment for positive reviews, and tools we don't like don't get recommended, period.</p>
